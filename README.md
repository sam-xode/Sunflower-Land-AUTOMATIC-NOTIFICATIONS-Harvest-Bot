## 👑 What This Bot Does

This bot keeps you in the loop about your Sunflower Land farms without having to constantly check the game! Never miss a harvest again!

### 🔥 Features

- **Farm Notifications** - Get pinged when your crops, resources, and crafts are ready!
- **Upcoming Harvests** - See what's coming up next on your farm
- **Multi-Farm Support** - Track multiple farms with one Discord account
- **Flexible Notifications** - Choose between DMs or channel notifications
- **Interactive UI** - Easy-to-use slash commands and fancy buttons

## 🚀 Getting Started


### 📱 Basic Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/register` | Register your farm for notifications |
| `/unregister` | Remove your farm from notifications |
| `/list_farms` | See all your registered farms |
| `/next_harvest` | Check what's ready (or will be soon) |

## ⚡ Command Guide

### Register Your Farm
```
/register farm_id:YOUR_FARM_ID notification_type:[DM or channel]
```

### Unregister Your Farm
```
/unregister farm_id:YOUR_FARM_ID
```

### List Your Farms
```
/list_farms
```

### Check Upcoming Harvests
```
/next_harvest
```
Then select which farm you want to check from the dropdown menu!

## 🔍 How It Works

The bot checks your farm every few minutes and sends you a notification when:
- 🌱 Crops are ready to harvest
- 🪓 Resources are ready to collect
- 🛠️ Crafting is complete


## 🛡️ Privacy & Security

- We only store your farm ID and notification preferences
- No personal information is collected
- Your data is never shared with third parties
- You can remove your data anytime with `/unregister`
